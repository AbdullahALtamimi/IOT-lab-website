What is IoT Lab and what services does it provide?
The Internet of Things Lab is an integrated laboratory environment that provides companies, developers and providers of new and existing systems with the tools, resources and expertise to provide not only the latest technology solutions but also allows them to match them with their peers and advise on how to bring their solutions to market. The lab contains solution presentations for developer groups for Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems and various other associated advanced technologies.
The main focus of the laboratory is to convert customer projects into products and commercial revenue. Where he runs all intensive workshops with training covering many areas of Internet of Things and Embedded Systems.
The lab can also be used to host networking and collaboration activities across the IoT community as well as multilateral sessions.
